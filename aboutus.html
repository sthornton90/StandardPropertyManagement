Standard Property Management History



Standard Property Management began as a phone conversation regarding the need for a good, trusted company to maintain investment properties. A few years later, the opportunity arose for that idea to come to life. Officially founded on 02/01/20, Standard Property Management is aimed to stay fluid and liquid, to always be able to serve our customers in any economic condition. Providing a cost effective alternative for landlords to maintain their property.


Company Core Values


1) Trust and Transparency
2) Professionalism
3) Servant Leadership
4) Individual Growth



Vision

Our vision, is to standardize the real estate investment experience, in terms of the owner/tenant relationship. Provide a template for the way property owners maintain their property, and the way tenants occupy the property. Provide a pathway for our contractors, employees, and associates to maintain a good life, and path to individual financial freedom.



Mission

Real estate is, and always will be, one of the best asset classes for people to invest in. Standard Property Management Group LLC is dedicated to protecting and maintaining that investment for our customers, so that they may reap more of the rewards of their investment, and less of the headache.



Who We Are


We are a property maintenance company which provides a standard model for investment property maintenance.
We work for our clients while working with subcontractors.
Our goal is to save clients money, not spend money.
We are not real estate competitors, and have no desire to be.
Our goal is for our clients to achieve their goals real estate maintenance; their success is our success.
